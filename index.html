About Us

Welcome to Alani Mediterranean, nestled in the heart of Oregon, where we're passionate about bringing the authentic flavors of the Mediterranean to your table. Our food cart is not just a family-owned gem, but a vibrant gathering spot for the community.

As first-generation Arab-Americans, we believe in the power of food to connect people and cultures. Our Halal dishes are more than just meals; they are expressions of love and a celebration of togetherness. Join us at Alani, where every dish is an invitation to experience joy and unity, one delightful bite at a time.
